Budgeting and Forecasting

Budgeting and financial forecasting are tools that businesses and individual use to establish a plan for where either management would like to take their company, or individuals planning out their future financial goals.

Budgeting: Quantifies the expectation of revenue that a business or individual would like to achieve in a future period

Forecasting: Estimates the amount of revenue or income that will be achieved in a future period.
