About Us


Qudsia Butt, CEO

Qudsia Butt, CPA, MBAcc is the founder & CEO of Q Accounting Services. She is a highly experienced Certified Public Accountant with over 15 years’ experience in both Canada and the United States.

Having earned her Bachelor’s and MBA in Accounting from Seattle, Washington and her vast experiences with corporations like Microsoft, Qudsia as a Certified Public Accountant of Ontario is passionate about creating, building and growing your business.

As an experienced accountant she is helping businesses and individuals grow by providing Accounting/Bookkeeping, Tax with a focus on their financial goals and aspirations.

“My goal has always been to have a proactive and informed approach to the needs of my clients.”

Professional, accurate and friendly, she is ready to help her clients thrive!
