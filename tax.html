Corporate Income Tax Returns

All resident corporations (except tax-exempt Crown corporations, Hutterite colonies and registered charities) have to file a corporation income tax (T2) return every tax year even if there is no tax payable. This includes: non-profit organizations. tax-exempt corporations. inactive corporations.

WSIB

WSIB is the Provincial agency that provides support and insurance to employees for loss of wages and other benefits to employees in the event of a work-related accident or illness. Injured employees are compensated through an accident fund which is financed by employers through an assessment process. Each regular employer must file an annual statement of payroll with Worker's Compensation.
